Professional Biography

My name is Abdalrhman Salah Ibrahim. My journey into the world of cybersecurity began with a simple curiosity about how systems work and, more importantly, how they can be broken to be made stronger.

I enrolled in the intensive specialized tracks at MaharaTech (ITI), focusing on the offensive side of security. I successfully completed the Penetration Testing Professional track, learning methodologies from reconnaissance to exploitation.

To ensure a solid foundation, I earned the Computer Network Fundamentals certification, gaining deep understanding of TCP/IP and OSI models. I also expanded into digital investigation by studying Forensic Medicine at Misr University for Science and Technology.
