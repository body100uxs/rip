MY JOURNEY
Professional Biography

My name is Abdalrhman Salah Ibrahim. My journey into the world of cybersecurity began with a simple curiosity about how systems work and, more importantly, how they can be broken to be made stronger. I am not just a student; I am a practitioner who believes in the hands-on approach to security.

To build a solid foundation, I enrolled in the intensive specialized tracks at MaharaTech (ITI). My primary focus was mastering the offensive side of security. I successfully completed the Penetration Testing Professional track, where I learned the methodologies of ethical hacking, from reconnaissance to exploitation and reporting.

Understanding that security is built on top of networks, I ensured my groundwork was solid by earning the Computer Network Fundamentals certification, also from MaharaTech. This gave me the deep understanding of TCP/IP, OSI models, and routing necessary to analyze network traffic and spot anomalies.

My passion didn't stop there. I expanded my knowledge into digital investigation by studying Forensic Medicine in Crime Detection at Misr University for Science and Technology, giving me a unique perspective on evidence handling and investigative procedures.

Currently, I am expanding my expertise by pursuing the Cisco Cybersecurity certification to further validate my skills in securing enterprise environments.
